    Within this dialog you are able to define the source you like to extract. It is possible to extract tables from Cloudera. In order to get a working application please make at least a selection for all fields. The explanation will guide you through the selection steps.

    • green values: These are the selected values
    • white values: These are values which match to your selection
    • grey values: There values doesn't match to your selection

      Define a Limit

      The limit-function helps you to limit the amount of data you retrieve from cloudera. Especially for prototyping it could help you to save time and ressources. If you want to remove the limit-function just delete "LIMIT VALUE" from your script.

      Table and Column Details

      On the left side you can discover your available tables. This table helps you to get a better understandig how many rows of data your tables are containing. On the right side you can discover the column view. It shows you how many distinct values your columns contain and which data-type they have.
